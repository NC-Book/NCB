\exercise{From case-wise to Kronecker and back\label{exFriendsNotation}}{1}
Write the following case-wise distributions using the Kronecker delta notation:

\eqn{
  \mbox{a)}\quad p_k = \left\{ \begin{array}{l c l} 3/4 &~& \mbox{if $k=4$}  \\ 1/4 & & \mbox{if $k=8$} \\ 0 & & \mbox{otherwise} \end{array}\right.}
\eqn{
\mbox{b)}\quad
p_k = \left\{ \begin{array}{l c l} 1/3 &~& \mbox{if $k=3$}  \\
1/3 & & \mbox{if $k=4$} \\ 
1/3 & & \mbox{if $k=14$} \\
0 & & \mbox{otherwise} \end{array}\right.
}
Then, write the following distributions using the case-wise notation:
\eqn{
\mbox{c)}\quad p_k=\frac16 \delta_{k,1} + \frac13 \delta_{k,2} + \frac12 \delta_{k,3}\quad\quad\quad \mbox{d)}\quad p_k=\frac15 \delta_{k,5} + \frac45 \delta_{k,10} 
}

\solution
For part (a) we find 
\eq{
p_k=\frac34 \delta_{k,4} + \frac14 \delta_{k,8},
}
and similarly for (b),
\eq{
p_k=\frac13 \delta_{k,3} + \frac13 \delta_{k,4} + \frac13 \delta_{k,14}.
}
Now we are going backwards and convert to case-wise notation. For (c) we get
\eq{
p_k = \left\{ \begin{array}{l c l} 1/5 &~& \mbox{if $k=5$}  \\
4/3 & & \mbox{if $k=10$} \\ 
0 & & \mbox{otherwise} \end{array}\right. .
}
Likewise for (d),
\eq{
p_k = \left\{ \begin{array}{l c l} 1/6 &~& \mbox{if $k=1$}  \\
1/3 & & \mbox{if $k=2$} \\ 
1/2 & & \mbox{if $k=3$} \\
0 & & \mbox{otherwise} \end{array}\right. .
}
