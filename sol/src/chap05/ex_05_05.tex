
\exercise{Social diameter of Oldenburg\label{exSWiceland}}{2}
The city of Oldenburg has a population of 160,000. Let's assume that the mean degree of the acquaintance network is $z=120$ and the clustering coefficient is $c=0.2$. Estimate the social diameter of Oldenburg. 

\solution
We can use our formula for diameter with clustering
\eqa{
D&=&\log_{(1-c)z}((1-c)N) \\
 &=& \log_{96}(128,000) \\ 
 &\approx& 2.58
}
