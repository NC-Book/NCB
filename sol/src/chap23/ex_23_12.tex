\exercise{Kirchhoff proof}{5}
Find a forward-facing derivation of Kirchhoff's theorem and the matrix-tree theorem, i.e.~a line of reasoning that leads to the result, rather than postulating the result and then proving that it is true.

\solution
This is an unsolved problem. Kirchhoff's own work only states the theorem without proof, but several subsequent paper have provided proofs, showing that the theorem is true. However, these proofs do not provide much intuition that would motivate the theorem.
